### Yields
Tiles generate the following base yields:

- Science
- Production
- Wealth
- Food
- Faith

These are all given to the city that has ownership of the tile and is working the tile.
Tiles also "radiate"
- Stability Modifiers

- Great people points

### Modifiers
- Montains - Science 2x
- River - Food 2x (status: Irrigated)
